# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

The UniSSH project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**, as its
code of conduct. The full, canonical text is maintained by the Contributor
Covenant project and applies in its entirety:

- **Full text:** <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>
- **Plain-language FAQ:** <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/faq/>
- **Translations:** <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/translations/>

In short: everyone participating in this project's spaces — issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any other channel — is expected to help keep them
welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free, regardless of background or
identity. Unwelcome, abusive, discriminatory, or otherwise disruptive conduct
is not tolerated. Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying these
standards and may take fair corrective action (from a warning to a permanent
ban) in response to behavior they deem inappropriate, as described in the full
text linked above.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces and also when an individual is
officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior **privately** to the maintainer at
**`uni@goduni.me`**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly, and the reporter's privacy and security will be respected. Do not open a
public issue for a Code of Conduct concern.

> The maintainer is anonymous by design (see the project's privacy posture), but
> the reporting address above is monitored and is the correct channel for both
> conduct and [security](SECURITY.md) matters.
